### 2026-07-11 · Original concept

**Runway** — a cheeky solitaire deckbuilder about building a startup before
the cash runs out. Cards on a felt table; play one, resources tween, an event
fires. The clock is everything: Runway drains by Burn every turn; cross a
growth milestone and you raise a round — the only refill, and each raise sets
a higher bar. Neglect the product and Tech Debt piles up, incidents hit,
customers churn. Your business model isn't chosen — it *emerges* from the
cards you play (brand → DTC, paid flywheel → SaaS, sales team → B2B), and
investors grade you on the North Star that matches what you became. Pivots
are the comedy and the escape hatch. Manic optimism with a burn-clock ticking
underneath. Win big (Unicorn), win small (Ramen), or flame out gloriously —
and get a specific story every time.
