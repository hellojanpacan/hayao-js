Sound Studio

Every sound here is generated live by the hayao engine from a few dozen lines of data — no audio files, no samples. The music is deterministic and machine-verified: the same Song renders byte-identical PCM in Node, so it's tested like any other code.

synthesis: pure TypeScript · zero dependencies · rendered headlessly & feature-tested · played through the Web Audio API

    game soundtrack · background-first

    Palace Hours · Original Game Soundtrack

      ▶ play a cue to see its waveform & spectrogram

      Four looping cues — one per game state (title / explore / tension / reward). Unlike a listening album, this score is engineered to get out of the way: voices are lowpassed low and the master stays near-flat so the mix sits under ~2.6 kHz (the high band left open for bright SFX), and the arrangements stay sparse and un-brickwalled so a coin or blip pops over the bed. Proven headlessly — dark centroid + healthy crest factor are asserted in the test suite.
