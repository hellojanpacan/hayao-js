the store

Play the engine.

Twenty-eight games, every one machine-verified before it shipped. Four are art-finished in the house woodblock style; the rest are honest engine slices — each one built to stress one capability end to end, playable in the browser and readable at the source. This is what “make a game with hayao” produces today.
