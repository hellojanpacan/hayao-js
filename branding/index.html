Four directions for Hayao

One engine, four honest stances — each a coherent story, not a coat of paint.

What all four must carry: the truth that a Hayao game is a pure, deterministic function — provable, headless, and warm in its output. They differ only in which of those truths leads: the craft, the rigor, the architecture, or the heritage. Open each; they're self-contained HTML.
Direction A

Provable Garden

Evolves today's warm, hand-tended identity and adds one rigorous mark: the proof seal. Craft you can trust.

Leads with · craft + trust · lowest risk
Direction B

Deterministic

The engineering truth, undecorated: a terminal, a cursor, one signal-green accent, state-transition ribbons.

Leads with · rigor · most credible to devs
Direction C

The Observer

Makes the one idea the whole logo: a luminous pure core radiating frames to plugin observers.

Leads with · architecture · most ownable
Direction D

Woodblock

The woodblock as a deterministic function — carve once, print identical forever. Heritage as concept, handled with care.

Leads with · heritage + story · highest ceiling & care
