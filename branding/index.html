Four directions for Hayao

One engine, four honest stances — each a coherent story, not a coat of paint.

What all four must carry: the truth that a Hayao game is a pure, deterministic function — headless, machine-verifiable, and warm in its output. They differ only in which of those truths leads: the craft, the rigor, the agent-native benefit, or the heritage. Open each; they're self-contained HTML.

"Hayao" is used as an evocative, standalone name — not affiliated with or endorsed by any filmmaker or studio. Clear it with trademark counsel before launch.
