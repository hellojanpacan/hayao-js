motion

One gesture: the press.

Motion is scarce and physical — the ink meeting paper. The mark presses in once and holds. Nothing loops loudly; nothing floats. (Respects prefers-reduced-motion.)

The impression settles into place — the same place, every time. Determinism, felt.
