An engine that grows games like a garden — patient, hand-tended, alive — yet every leaf of it is machine-proven true.

The idea

The name honours a maker known for craft, patience, and worlds that feel hand-drawn. Direction A keeps that warmth — parchment, botanical line, a serif voice — and pairs it with a single rigorous mark: the proof seal. Warmth says made with care; the seal says and proven correct. Craft you can trust.

When this is right

Choose Provable Garden if Hayao's story is craft + trust: a warm, human engine whose secret weapon is rigor. It evolves today's parchment identity rather than replacing it — lowest risk, deepest continuity.

Watch out: serif-warm can read as "boutique / hobby". The mono + seal are what keep it credible to engineers — never drop them.
