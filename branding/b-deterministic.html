When this is right

Choose Deterministic if Hayao is sold to engineers and AI agents first. It is honest, dense, and instantly credible on Hacker News / GitHub. The identity is the product demo.

Watch out: it says nothing about the games being warm or joyful — it risks feeling cold, and it throws away the evocative name. Best if paired with lush game art doing the emotional work.
