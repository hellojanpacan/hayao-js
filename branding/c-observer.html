The idea

Direction C makes Hayao's one idea the whole logo: a luminous deterministic core radiating frames outward to plugin observers. It reads as a simulation / physics-engine brand — modern, a little astronomical, concept-first. The gradient (warm core → cool observers) encodes the architecture in a single glance.

When this is right

Choose The Observer to lead with the architectural insight — the thing that makes Hayao genuinely novel. Most memorable and ownable of the four; scales into motion, docs diagrams, and a talk deck effortlessly.

Watch out: "cosmic dark-mode SaaS" is a crowded look. The specific core→observer diagram is what keeps it from being generic — protect that mark; don't let it drift into a plain glowing orb.
