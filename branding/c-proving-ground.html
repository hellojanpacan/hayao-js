When this is right

Choose The Proving Ground to lead with Hayao's true, ownable differentiator — not "determinism" in the abstract, but the benefit: an agent can author a game and the engine can prove it. This speaks straight to the real buyer (AI agents + the engineers deploying them) and to founders shipping agent tooling.

Watch out: it stakes everything on the agent-authoring story being real and demoable — the passing gates must reflect actual verify runs, never decoration. Keep the numbers honest and reproducible.
