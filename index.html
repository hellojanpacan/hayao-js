hayao.js

An AI-first game engine. The game is a pure deterministic function; everything else is a plugin.

Sokoban →

Pure solver-provable logic + a scene-tree SVG view + undo. Every level is machine-verified winnable.

Emberreign →

A reign of decisions (Reigns-lite): four meters, double-edged cards, story arcs — judgement proven to matter (19/20 vs 0/20 reigns) and every doom proven honest.

Vellgrove Rally →

Top-down racing (Micro Machines-lite): understeer at speed, grass that punishes, cut-proof laps — braking proven faster than flooring it.

Pinshine →

A Peggle-ish physics board: swept-circle collision (no tunneling at any speed), restitution proven energy-honest, every board proven clearable by an aim-search bot.

Cadence Hollow →

A rhythm dungeon (NecroDancer-lite): move only on the pulse, foes dance in lockstep — the beat IS sim time, so even rhythm replays deterministically.

Tarnholm →

An island town builder (Islanders-lite): every roof scores by its neighbours, with the gain shown live — placement skill proven to nearly double random play.

Palewood →

One night of survival horror (Darkwood-lite): a raycast-lit lantern, panned growls in the dark, fuel pressure that forbids camping — the night proven survivable, the dark proven deadly.

Fernrow →

A farming year (Stardew-lite): four seasons, energy-budgeted days, crops that wither out of season — the festival goal proven reachable, reinvestment proven to compound.

Glimmerfall →

Match-3 (Puzzle Quest-ish): cascade combos, spring-tweened choreography over an instant sim — 100 boards proven fair, targets proven reachable.

Vantage →

Turn-based tactics (Into the Breach-lite): telegraphed attacks, push chains that rewrite the enemy's future — a perfect defence is machine-proven possible, and doing nothing proven fatal.

Thornspire →

A roguelike deckbuilder (Slay the Spire-lite): card DSL, telegraphed intents, drafts between fights — tuned to a proven 17/20 pilot win rate, with drafting proven to matter.

Hollowdeep →

A traditional roguelike: procgen floors proven connected across 50 seeds, raycast FOV with map memory, bump combat — every dungeon bot-proven winnable.

Bramblefall →

An RTS skirmish: flow-field pathfinding, 260+ units, a spear/cavalry/archer counter triangle — every counter edge is duel-proven and the war is bot-winnable.

Rootward →

Duskveil →

Emberwake →

Gleamvale →
